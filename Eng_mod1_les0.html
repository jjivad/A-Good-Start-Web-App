INTRODUCTION TO A GOOD START

Script

Welcome all to your first session of A Good start!

A Good Start is a life skills course that provides individuals with the skills needed for them to better participate in their local communities.

So, what are these Life Skills?
Life skills are psychosocial skills that make it easier to face life challenges and crises in healthy, realistic and positive ways. Life skills encourage wellbeing for oneself and others.

By the end of the course, we hope that you will have gained the core life skills which are:

  • Self confidence
  • Respecting yourself and others
  • Interpersonal skills, for example, empathy and compassion
  • Managing emotions
  • Personal responsibility (including dependability, integrity, and work ethics)
  • Positive attitude and self-motivation
  • Conflict management
  • Teamwork
  • Communication, that is, listening, verbal, and written communication
  • Cooperation and teamwork
  • Creative thinking
  • Critical thinking and problem solving
  • Decision making

Thank you for listening, that was a welcome session to A Good Start!
