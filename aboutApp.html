A Good Start is a life skills course that provides individuals with the skills needed for them to better participate in their local communities.

The content of this course has been made by Xavier project, a Cummunity led learning that enables refugees in Kenya and Uganda to lead fulfilled lives. UNHCR Innovation Award 2019 Winner

This course is delivered by different Community Based Organizations (CBOs) in Kakuma refugee camp such as SIR (Solidarity Initiative for refugees), URISE, RAI (Resilience Action International), and many other organizations partenering with Xavier project across the country (Kenya) and outside Kenya.
