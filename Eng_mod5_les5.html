Lesson 5: Dealing with sexual/gender conflicts

Script

Welcome to our fifth lesson on dealing with sexual/gender conflicts. Here we are going to talk about what types of sexual and gender conflicts exist within society as well ways and means that we can apply strategies and methodologies to deal with sexual and gender conflict. We will also reflect on emotional responses to sexual and gender conflict.

What does it mean to have sexual conflict?

This is when two sexes have conflicting optimal fitness strategies concerning reproduction, particularly over the mode and frequency of matting. To make it simpler, it is when males may benefit from the multiple matings and females may not. Therefore, males may be more likely to benefit from having more sexual partners than females can.
